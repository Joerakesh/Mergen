Guidelines

Submission Guidelines

1. Authors are advised to carefully see and follow the instructions and guidelines before submission of papers.

2. Papers and articles must be submitted within the deadline.

3. Authors are advised to strictly follow the MLA Handbook – IX Edition for in-text citations, notes, and references.

4. All contributions must be sent through: mergensjcenglish@gmail.com. Personal submission using any other email address is not accepted.

5. Authors are required to conduct plagiarism verification by themselves. Only URKUND or TURNITIN plagiarism verification reports are accepted. The original scanned plagiarism verification report must be submitted along with the paper.

6. Papers are selected for publication based on merit, topic relevance, and writing skill.

7. All contributions must be original, not published elsewhere, and cannot be sent to other journals simultaneously.

8. Full-length academic papers should be 4000-4500 words, including notes and references. Commentary & Perspective should be 2000-2500 words, including notes and references. Book reviews should be within 2000 words. Word limits must be strictly followed.

9. Contributions must be submitted in MS Word (.docx), using Times New Roman font, size 12 pt, line spacing 1.5, paragraph spacing 0 pt (before) & 6 pt (after). Page margins must be 15mm (0.59”) on all four sides.

10. Spell words in British style.

11. Use single quotation marks to quote a sentence or statement. Use double quotation marks only when quoting a statement within another statement that uses single quotation marks.

12. Authors must provide their name, designation, email address, and contact number at the end of the paper.

13. The Editor reserves the right to reject any article or publish it in a later edition of the journal.

14. If required, contributors will be asked to provide hard copies of their papers/articles/essays.

15. Articles and essays will not be published if they contain misbehavior, undesirable words, or pressure for publication.

Formatting and Submission

• Font: 12-point type - Times New Roman

• Margins: 15mm on all sides

• Page Numbers: Sequentially numbered pages

• Line Spacing: 1.5

• Citation Style: MLA Handbook – IX Edition

• Plagiarism Report: Only URKUND or TURNITIN reports are accepted

Word Count Guidelines

• Academic Papers: 4000-4500 words

• Commentary & Perspective: 2000-2500 words

• Book Review: Up to 2000 words
